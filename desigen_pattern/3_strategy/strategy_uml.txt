CheckoutService --> PaymentStrategy


==============================
2. FLOW
==============================

Client
  |
  v
Choose payment strategy
  |
  v
Pass strategy to CheckoutService
  |
  v
CheckoutService calls strategy.pay(amount)


==============================
3. INTERVIEW NOTES
==============================

Intent:
- encapsulate interchangeable algorithms
- switch behavior at runtime

Benefits:
- removes if/else chains
- easy to extend new strategies
- follows Open/Closed Principle
